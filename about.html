About

Multidisciplinary designer with a focus on publication design, poster design, typography, and image-making. Versatile, prolific, detail-oriented, and interested in highly researched outcomes and the sophisticated aesthetic codes of legacy design.

First Class graduate from BA Communication Design at the Glasgow School of Art, with a background in Humanities at the Sorbonne, Paris. Shortlisted for the Penguin Cover Design Award. Currently a Resident at the Caseroom @ SWG3 in Glasgow, Scotland.

Freelance Work and Commissions re-open in February 2025.
